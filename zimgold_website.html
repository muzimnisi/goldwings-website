About ZimGold

ZimGold Capital FZ LLC is a Dubai-based company using blockchain technology to fund and digitize gold mining operations in Zimbabwe. Through the ZGUT utility token and ZGS gold-backed stablecoin, we connect real-world gold with decentralized finance, offering transparency, liquidity, and investor control.

Our Tokens

ZimGold Utility Token (ZGUT) funds mining development and grants early investor privileges, including discounted conversion into our stablecoin.

ZimGold Stablecoin (ZGS) is 100% backed by physical gold and redeemable at 1 ZGS = 1 gram of gold.
